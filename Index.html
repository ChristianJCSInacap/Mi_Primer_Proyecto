We are a world wide bussines oriented to provide the best prices for the best products. In Aeki, the customer is the boss, we love to make our customer happy giving them exactly what they want! Wild furniture season is near!

This year, wild furniture season will begin in May 1st, every year we are blessed with beautiful views of the forest forniture (or how we like to call it Foresture) like the one in the left, after a long winter the little furni-seeds are ready to blosson becomming complex and pretty useful pieces ready to become part of your home, you can visit this nature wonder and sit on a newly grown chair or apreciate the high view climbing a vine ladder, just be carefull with the growing chandeliers in the trees, they could be very dangerous when the wind blows, remember to be cautious and listen to the wood bells that hang in the trees.
